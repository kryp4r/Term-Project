Magsin De Marque


       Magasin De Marue was founded in 2022 by 2 university students from American University of Phnom Penh. Magasin de marque is a French inspired clothing brand. We imported all kinds of high-quality products from big brands such as Balenciaga, Alexander McQueen, Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Dior etc.
